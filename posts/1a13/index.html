二、密码学 / 安全方向公式

RSA

$$
n = pq,\quad \varphi(n)=(p-1)(q-1)
$$

$$
c \equiv m^e \pmod n,\quad m \equiv c^d \pmod n
$$

ECC

$$
y^2 = x^3 + ax + b \pmod p
$$

$$
Q = kP
$$

Hash

$$
h = H(m),\quad H(m_1)=H(m_2)
$$

Birthday Attack

$$
P \approx 1 - e^{-k^2/2N}
$$

$$
y = Xw + b

$$
\theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta \nabla L

Softmax

$$
\text{softmax}(x_i)=\frac{e^{x_i}}{\sum_j e^{x_j}}
